Noor is a way to connect with your students...

to enhance the student's experience with the platform.

About Us

Noor is a child company of EldarTech, the two-person father/son founded company whose CEO is Hazem Eldarymli.
Noor's mission is to give an experience that differs from others. We want to build our company off of easy to use UI and UX, and we want our customers and their students to have the best experience with our application.
